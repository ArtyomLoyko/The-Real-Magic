ABOUT

"THE REAL MAGIC" is a game in which you have to help the main character kill all the monsters and save mankind. This game will be useful to you. In the process, you can improve your skills in arithmetic, English and geography.

You have to solve small problems and get for them the magic with which you can kill monsters, saving mankind from imminent death. The better your knowledge, the easier it will be for you to do it.

Mankind needs your help!!!
Click on "Start Game!" and save it as quickly as possible along with the real magic!
